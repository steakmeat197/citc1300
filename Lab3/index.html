Berserk

Love

Family and sacrifice was what is known of The Band of the Hawk. Led by there fearless leader Griffith and second in command Casca they head confidently into battles assuring victory with thier top gun leading the charge....Guts. He tears through soldier after soldier with reckless abandon.

Loss

Betrayed by the only person he ever called his friend Griffith. Griffith sacrificed the only family Guts has ever known and trusted and became the powerful Femto and fifth member of the God Hand.

Revenge

The Brand of Sacrifice etched in his neck and the Berserk Armor diggin into his flesh Guts sets out on a journey to restore Cascas mind and get revenge on Griffith for taking away all he has ever loved. Will Guts meet new companions on his journey or will he die fighting alone.
